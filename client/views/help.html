Step 1

Choose a username

The username is what is shown to the others on the same room and it doesn't have to be unique


Step 2

Create room / Enter room

In this step, you either create a room or enter in another's room


Step 3 (create)

Share the code with your friends

Copy the room's code and share it with your friends so that they can watch together with you


Step 3 (enter)

Enter your friends code

Paste the code your friend sent you



Step 4

Enter the same website

Either click the link next to your friend's name or enter it manually


Finished!

Now your video players will be synced

Everytime any of you pause, play or jump the video, this will be reflected on everyone else's video player as well
